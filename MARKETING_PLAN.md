# 🚀 Callyy - End-to-End Marketing Plan

## Zero to First 1000 Customers Strategy

**Product**: Callyy - AI Voice Agent Platform for Indian Businesses
**Target Launch**: Q4 2025
**Primary Channels**: LinkedIn, Twitter (X), Threads

---

## 📊 Executive Summary

Callyy is an AI-powered voice agent platform designed for Indian businesses to automate phone calls with intelligent, conversational AI assistants. With features like multilingual support, customer memory systems, and pay-as-you-go pricing in INR, Callyy is uniquely positioned for the Indian market.

---

## 🎯 Target Audience Personas

### 1. **Startup Founder (Primary)**
- **Age**: 25-40
- **Company Size**: 5-50 employees
- **Pain Points**: 
  - Limited customer support bandwidth
  - High cost of hiring call center staff
  - Scaling customer communication
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn (primary), Twitter (X)
- **Content Preferences**: Success stories, ROI metrics, product demos

### 2. **SMB Business Owner**
- **Age**: 30-55
- **Industry**: E-commerce, Healthcare, Real Estate, Insurance
- **Pain Points**:
  - Missed calls = Lost business
  - After-hours customer inquiries
  - Language barriers with customers
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn, Threads
- **Content Preferences**: How-to guides, case studies, testimonials

### 3. **Tech Lead / CTO**
- **Age**: 28-45
- **Company Size**: 10-200 employees
- **Pain Points**:
  - API integration complexity
  - Building vs. buying voice AI
  - Data security concerns
- **Platforms**: Twitter (X), LinkedIn
- **Content Preferences**: Technical docs, API showcases, architecture posts

### 4. **Customer Success / Operations Manager**
- **Age**: 25-40
- **Pain Points**:
  - Manual customer follow-ups
  - Tracking customer context across calls
  - Agent training time
- **Platforms**: LinkedIn, Threads
- **Content Preferences**: Workflow automation, productivity hacks
